Principal’s Desk

Dear Parents and Little Learners,

Welcome to Aarambh Foundation, where every child’s journey of discovery begins! As Principal, I feel honored to be part of a community that values Quality, Commitment, and Care, and is dedicated to creating a space where little minds grow with confidence, curiosity, and compassion.

Our goal is to provide a nurturing and joyful environment where children feel safe to explore, ask questions, and express themselves. We believe that learning should be an adventure—filled with play, creativity, and meaningful experiences that build not only knowledge but also self-esteem and empathy.

At our preschool, we focus on holistic development—encouraging physical, emotional, social, and cognitive growth through fun, hands-on activities. We partner with families to ensure that each child’s individual strengths are celebrated, while gently guiding them toward new challenges and discoveries.

Together, we are shaping the future of India—one child at a time. I invite you to join us in this beautiful journey, where laughter, learning, and love come together to create lasting memories and lifelong skills.

Warmly,
Sonali Sengar
Principal, AARAMBH FOUNDATION
