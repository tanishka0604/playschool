AARAMBH FOUNDATION

For Kids Program

Play School

Our Play School program is designed for toddlers to explore the world around them in a playful and supportive setting. Through interactive games, music, storytelling, and creative activities, children develop early learning skills, coordination, and social interaction. The focus is on fostering curiosity, imagination, and a love for learning in a safe and joyful environment.

Daycare

Our Day Care service provides a caring space for children where parents can be assured of their child’s well-being throughout the day. Along with nutritious meals, rest, and structured activities, children learn essential life skills and build routines that promote emotional security, independence, and confidence—all while being supported by trained caregivers who prioritize each child’s needs.

Nursery

The Nursery program bridges the transition from home to a structured learning environment. It introduces foundational concepts through fun activities, sensory experiences, and guided learning. Emphasis is placed on language development, motor skills, social interaction, and emotional growth while encouraging children to express themselves freely and confidently.

LKG

In LKG, children are introduced to early academic skills in a warm and interactive setting. The curriculum focuses on basic literacy, numeracy, shapes, colors, and storytelling, helping children develop attention, communication, and reasoning skills. Activities are designed to encourage creativity, curiosity, and cooperative learning while building a sense of responsibility and self-discipline.

UKG

Our UKG program prepares children for primary school through structured learning combined with experiential activities. Children develop critical thinking, problem-solving, and fine motor skills through projects, games, and hands-on exercises. We focus on building confidence, leadership, and social skills while ensuring that learning remains engaging and fun.
