Director’s Desk

Welcome to Aarambh Foundation Pre-School

At our preschool, we believe that every child is a unique spark of curiosity, creativity, and potential. Our mission is to create an environment where little minds grow—a place where learning is joyful, exploration is encouraged, and each child is guided with Quality, Commitment, and Care.

We are dedicated to nurturing the future leaders, thinkers, and compassionate citizens of future India. Our approach combines play-based learning, hands-on activities, and emotional development to build a strong foundation for academic success and life skills. We value every child’s individuality and work closely with parents to ensure holistic growth, confidence, and self-expression.

As educators and caregivers, we are committed to providing a safe, stimulating, and supportive space where children feel loved, respected, and empowered to learn at their own pace. Together, with your trust and involvement, we aim to shape a generation that is thoughtful, creative, and socially responsible.

Thank you for being a part of our journey. We look forward to growing, learning, and celebrating every child’s milestones with you!

Warm regards,
Ravi Singh Tomar
Director, Aarambh Foundation
