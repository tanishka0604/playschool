Welcome to Aarambh’s Teacher Training Program

At Aarambh Preschool, we believe that every child’s journey of learning begins with love, curiosity, and confidence. Our teachers are guides, mentors, and role models who help shape the personalities and potential of our young learners. This program empowers educators with the right skills, mindset, and tools to create a nurturing, engaging, and joyful environment.

Our Vision

“To build a generation of confident, curious, and compassionate learners by empowering educators to create safe, inclusive, and stimulating classrooms where little minds grow.”

Program Objectives

Child-Centric Learning

Support children’s emotional, cognitive & social growth.

Interactive Teaching

Playful, activity-based, and inquiry-driven methods.

Positive Management

Build trust and foster a supportive classroom culture.

Parent Collaboration

Ensure parents are part of their child’s journey.

Health & Well-being

Focus on hygiene, nutrition, and mental health.

Continuous Learning

Encourage professional growth & reflection.
